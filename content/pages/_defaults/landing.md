# Stay Current With the Literature That Matters

A weekly AI-curated digest of practice-changing clinical publications from PubMed — delivered to your inbox, filtered by the topics you care about.

## What You Get

- **Weekly digests** of the most clinically relevant new publications
- **Structured summaries** — research question, key finding, design, limitations — so you can assess relevance in seconds
- **Topic filtering** — subscribe to the clinical areas that matter to your practice
- **Permanent archive** — browse past digests anytime on this site

## How to Subscribe

Create a free account to choose your clinical topic preferences and start receiving your personalized weekly digest.

## Built for Clinicians

Our pipeline is designed by domain experts. AI handles the volume; clinical judgment guides the curation. Every summary includes a feedback link so you can help us improve.
