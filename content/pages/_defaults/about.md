# About

This site delivers a weekly curated digest of practice-changing clinical literature from PubMed. Our automated pipeline searches, filters, and summarizes recent publications so you can stay current without reading every abstract.

## How It Works

1. **Search** — Each week, we query PubMed for recent publications matching our clinical focus areas using MeSH terms and targeted journal searches.
2. **Filter** — A two-pass system (rule-based + AI triage) narrows hundreds of results to the handful most relevant to clinical practice.
3. **Summarize** — An AI generates structured summaries highlighting research questions, key findings, study design, and limitations.
4. **Deliver** — Summaries are published here and delivered to subscribers via personalized email digests filtered by topic preference.

## For Clinicians, By Clinicians

The search terms, filter rules, and summarization prompts are designed and maintained by domain experts. AI assists with scale; clinical judgment guides the process.

## Feedback

Every article includes a feedback link. Your input helps us improve the pipeline's accuracy and relevance.
