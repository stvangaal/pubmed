## Information We Collect

When you subscribe to our digest service, we collect:

- **Email address** — required to deliver your weekly digest
- **Display name** — used to personalize your digest
- **Clinical topic preferences** — used to filter articles relevant to your interests

We do not collect payment information, browsing history, or device identifiers.

## How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

1. Deliver your personalized weekly literature digest
2. Allow you to manage your subscription and topic preferences
3. Respond to your feedback on individual articles

We do not sell, share, or rent your personal information to third parties.

## Data Storage

Your account information is stored on our WordPress hosting platform. Email delivery is handled by Resend. Both services maintain industry-standard security practices.

## Your Rights

You may at any time:

- **Update** your topic preferences via your account profile
- **Unsubscribe** by deleting your account or contacting us
- **Request deletion** of all your personal data by emailing us

## Cookies

Our site uses only essential cookies required for login and session management. We do not use analytics or advertising cookies.
