# About Stroke Conversations

Stroke Conversations delivers a weekly curated digest of practice-changing stroke literature from PubMed. Our automated pipeline searches, filters, and summarizes recent publications so stroke clinicians can stay current without reading every abstract.

## How It Works

1. **Search** — Each week, we query PubMed for recent stroke publications using MeSH terms covering acute treatment, prevention, rehabilitation, hospital care, imaging, and epidemiology.
2. **Filter** — A two-pass system (rule-based + AI triage) narrows hundreds of results to the handful most relevant to stroke clinical practice.
3. **Summarize** — An AI generates structured summaries highlighting research questions, key findings, study design, and limitations.
4. **Deliver** — Summaries are published here and delivered to subscribers via personalized email digests filtered by topic preference.

## Curated By

Dr. Stephen van Gaal — the search terms, filter rules, and summarization prompts are designed and maintained by stroke domain experts. AI assists with scale; clinical judgment guides the process.
