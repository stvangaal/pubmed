# Stay Current With the Stroke Literature That Matters

A weekly AI-curated digest of practice-changing stroke publications from PubMed — delivered to your inbox, filtered by the topics you care about.

## What You Get

- **Weekly digests** of the most clinically relevant new stroke publications
- **Structured summaries** — research question, key finding, design, limitations — so you can assess relevance in seconds
- **Topic filtering** — choose from Acute Treatment, Prevention, Rehabilitation, Hospital Care, Imaging, and Epidemiology
- **Permanent archive** — browse past digests anytime on this site

## How to Subscribe

Create a free account to choose your clinical topic preferences and start receiving your personalized weekly stroke digest.

## Built for Stroke Clinicians

Curated by Dr. Stephen van Gaal. Our pipeline is designed by stroke experts. AI handles the volume; clinical judgment guides the curation. Every summary includes a feedback link so you can help us improve.
